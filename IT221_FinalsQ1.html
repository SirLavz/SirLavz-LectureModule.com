Interactive Database Query Quiz

Selection (σ) and Projection (π) Practice

How to Play: Read the query requirement, draft your SQL and Algebra on a piece of paper, then click the button to reveal the answer. Note: SQL uses != and Algebra uses <> for inequality.
Source: Customers 1. Find customers who are NOT in 'California' OR have a country of 'Canada'.
SQL: SELECT * FROM Customers WHERE StateProvince <> 'California' OR Country = 'Canada';
Algebra: σStateProvince <> 'California' ∨ Country = 'Canada'(Customers)
Source: Products 2. Find products with a Price < 5.00 but an UnitsInStock > 50.
SQL: SELECT * FROM Products WHERE Price < 5.00 AND UnitsInStock > 50;
Algebra: σPrice < 5.00 ∧ UnitsInStock > 50(Products)
Source: Orders 3. Find orders by CustomerID 5 or 12, where TotalAmount is < 100.00.
SQL: SELECT * FROM Orders WHERE (CustomerID = 5 OR CustomerID = 12) AND TotalAmount < 100.00;
Algebra: σ(CustomerID = 5 ∨ CustomerID = 12) ∧ TotalAmount < 100(Orders)
Source: OrderDetails 4. Find order details where Quantity > 5 and the Discount is NOT 0.
SQL: SELECT * FROM OrderDetails WHERE Quantity > 5 AND Discount != 0;
Algebra: σQuantity > 5 ∧ Discount <> 0(OrderDetails)
Source: Orders 5. Find orders where ShippedDate is BETWEEN '2024-09-01' and '2024-09-30' AND FreightCost > 15.00.
SQL: SELECT * FROM Orders WHERE ShippedDate BETWEEN '2024-09-01' AND '2024-09-30' AND FreightCost > 15.00;
Algebra: σShippedDate ≥ '2024-09-01' ∧ ShippedDate ≤ '2024-09-30' ∧ FreightCost > 15(Orders)
Source: OrderDetails 6. Find order details where Unit Price > 10.00 AND Quantity is 10.
SQL: SELECT * FROM OrderDetails WHERE UnitPrice > 10.00 AND Quantity = 10;
Algebra: σUnitPrice > 10.00 ∧ Quantity = 10(OrderDetails)
Source: Customers 7. Find customers in 'New York' with an ID greater than 100.
SQL: SELECT * FROM Customers WHERE City = 'New York' AND CustomerID > 100;
Algebra: σCity = 'New York' ∧ CustomerID > 100(Customers)
